RETRACK

How can modern technology support remote rehabilitation for athletes?

That was the main topic in this project at the third semester. To tackle exactly this problem, my group mates and I developed RETRACK. Trackers that help athletes to recover from injuries with remote guidance.

With the help of paperprototypes, wireframing and designing a moodboard, we finished a design concept.

To help the patient with remote recovery and send the data to their medical assistant, we developed an idea of Trackers. The Trackerkit involves 2-5 Trackers, which can be put on the body with tape.

These trackers measure blood pressure, heart rate, body movement & posture and sleep of the patient. The data is collected and stored in the app so that the patient and the doctor can always see their rehabilitation process.

PROTOTYPING

To get in touch with first interface designs, we created paperprototypes. This helped to get user feedback early when testing and to solve design problems before the implementation even begins.

We wanted to not only help the patient to recover but also to cope with the medical assistant, we decided to design a patient and a doctor interface. The patient uses the wearable trackers and a mobile application and the doctor works with a webapplication including a dashboard and quickactions.

WIREFRAMING

Patient Wireframes

Wireframing plays a vital role in our development. Providing a framework that outlines the app`s structure and functionality. It helped us to ensure a thoughtful and user-centric approach to creating a digital tool that aids in rehabilitation. We created both patient and doctor wireframes, to help to visualize the flow of the applications, how users will navigate between different sections, set personal goals, and monitor their progress.

Patient Wireframes

On the doctor`s wireframes it was especially important to map out prescription tools, test result views, and a secure messaging system. Insuring to help with the patient managing system and to save time and ressources. We decided on a main dashboard and quickactions.

SCREENDESIGN

Patient

Remote rehabilitation, learning exercises and preventing further injuries are the main tasks of the patient application. To ensure this, the patient should be guided daily and always have an overview of their results when working out. The app helps with adjusting the trackers to have a overall guided and safe process.



Doctor

The doctor's web application helps in patient and time management, elevating patient care and enhancing rehabilitation outcomes. Featuring a personalized dashboard, it offers a quick overview of all current patients and adjustment of their rehabilitation plans. Additionally, we added a quick and efficient process for adding new patients with a strong focus on providing valuable assistance, to be a relieving and supportive tool for medical care professionals.
